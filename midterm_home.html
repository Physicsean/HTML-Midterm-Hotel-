Welcome

Snow Dreams Resort is located in the Snowy Shore of Lake Cold. Open November through March, we offer wintertime fun at amazing prices! Enjoy activities, relaxing, eaing, and exploring. Need a break from the kids, drop them off at our exclusive kids club!
